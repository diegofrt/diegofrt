## 👋 About Me
I am a Biomedical Engineer and PhD Candidate in Industrial & Systems Engineering, currently serving as Head of Information Technology at the National Cancer Institute of Chile. My work focuses on improving healthcare system performance through AI, advanced analytics, and operations research. I design and implement data-driven solutions to optimize cancer care delivery, reduce waiting times, and support evidence-based hospital decisions. I am especially interested in bridging clinical realities with digital innovation to scale resilient, high-impact healthcare systems.
